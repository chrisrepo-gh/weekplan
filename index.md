# Solo Week Activity Plan - Erlangen/Nuremberg
2026-07-20 to 2026-07-26

## Cultural & Leisure
- Visit the Schlossgarten in Erlangen for a relaxing evening walk.
- Explore the Nuremberg Castle (Kaiserburg) and the historic Old Town.
- Visit the Germanisches Nationalmuseum in Nuremberg.

## Outdoor & Fitness
- Cycling along the Regnitz river path.
- Swimming at the Wöhrder See in Nuremberg.
- Hiking in the Franconian Switzerland (Fränkische Schweiz) region.

## Dining & Social
- Try authentic Franconian cuisine at a local 'Biergarten' in Erlangen.
- Coffee and cake at a traditional café in Nuremberg's Handwerkerhof.
