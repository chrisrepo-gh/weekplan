# Solo Week Activity Plan - Erlangen/Nuremberg
2026-07-20 to 2026-07-26

## Cultural & Urban Exploration
* Visit the Nuremberg Imperial Castle (Kaiserburg) for historical insights and city views.
* Explore the Germanisches Nationalmuseum in Nuremberg.
* Stroll through the Schlossgarten in Erlangen for a relaxing afternoon.

## Nature & Recreation
* Cycling along the Regnitz river path.
* Swimming at the Dechsendorfer Weiher.
* Hiking in the Franconian Switzerland region (Fränkische Schweiz).

## Dining & Leisure
* Enjoy a traditional Franconian dinner in a local beer garden in Erlangen.
* Visit a specialty coffee shop in Nuremberg's Old Town.
* Browse the weekly market at Erlangen's Schlossplatz.
