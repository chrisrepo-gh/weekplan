# Solo Week Activity Plan: Erlangen & Nuremberg

Date Range: 2026-07-20 to 2026-07-26

## Cultural & Historical Exploration
- **Nuremberg Imperial Castle:** Spend a morning exploring the Kaiserburg and the historical city center.
- **Albrecht Dürer's House:** Visit the former home and workshop of the famous German Renaissance artist.
- **Documentation Center Nazi Party Rally Grounds:** A somber but essential educational visit in Nuremberg.

## Nature & Relaxation
- **Erlangen Schlossgarten:** Enjoy a relaxing stroll through the Baroque gardens in the heart of Erlangen.
- **Regnitz Cycling:** Rent a bike and cycle along the scenic Regnitz river paths connecting Erlangen and Nuremberg.
- **Fränkische Schweiz:** Take a short trip to the nearby 'Franconian Switzerland' for some hiking among unique rock formations.

## Gastronomy & Evening Entertainment
- **Erlangen Beer Gardens:** Spend an evening at a traditional local 'Keller' for a cold beer and local Franconian cuisine.
- **Nuremberg Old Town Dining:** Explore the Weinstadel area for a scenic dinner by the Pegnitz river.
- **Museum Night/Cinema:** Check the local listings at the Cinecittà Nuremberg for international film screenings.
